AnimatedShorts

A RubyOnRails app featuring three animated shorts using the Wistia Video API, an embedded Twitter Widget and integrating with the Google Map API. I used MaterializeCSS material design to style the web page and deployed to Heroku.

PupMunch Monthly

My most recent website, a subscription box for dog treats! Built utilizing the Primitive SASS framework. I configured the variables, mixins & styles to match my needs. Utilizing Gulp to spin up a web server, compile SASS to CSS, refresh the browser via Browsersync & optimize all assets (css,js,fonts, & images) for production. The site is connected to Moonclerk/Stripe to collect payments. KickoffLabs/MailChimp was used for collecting/sending emails during prelaunch.
